#  SeaShield AI
### Smart Fisherman Border Alert & Risk Prediction System using Agentic AI and MCP

> **"Saving Lives Beyond Borders"**

SeaShield AI is an **Agentic AI-powered maritime safety platform** designed to protect fishermen from accidental international border crossings and ocean-related risks. The system combines **real-time GPS tracking, AI risk prediction, offline navigation, family monitoring, emergency response, and intelligent AI agents** to create a safer and smarter fishing ecosystem.

Built using **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** architecture, SeaShield enables AI agents to interact with multiple tools, data sources, and services to provide proactive safety recommendations and decision support.

---

##  Problem Statement

Every day, thousands of fishermen venture into the sea to earn a livelihood. However, due to:

- Invisible maritime boundaries
- Lack of real-time navigation assistance
- Poor internet connectivity offshore
- Sudden weather changes
- Limited access to emergency support

many fishermen unknowingly cross international borders, resulting in:

- Arrests by foreign coast guards
- Boat confiscation
- Financial loss
- Emotional distress for families

Current solutions are expensive, hardware-dependent, and often unusable in remote sea regions.
